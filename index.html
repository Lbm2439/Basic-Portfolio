About Me


After alot of years wondering around this planet confused, I think I found something I really enjoy, Coding! It all started on a bored summer day in San Diego, California. I found myself drinking wine and building puzzles. I became so addicted to puzzles, it almost ruined my social life. Kidding, point is, I was THAT bored! I had a degree in Graphic Deisgn and Photography that I used, but only when jobs became availabe. That field of work was becoming so saturated and jobs were highly competitive and people no longer wanted to pay for services and believe it or not, San Diego is outrageously expensive, and it looked like, I was going to need a few million to get by. Kidding, again. I decided to create a side hustle to make extra cash and make work not feel so much like work. I opened a business, clothing to be exact. I created every piece of advertising graphic, logo, website, etc, all alone in my living room. With the help of a large amount of coffee and Adobe Creative Suite, my empire grew! I think somewhere around this point I saw myself doing more web design. Upon moving back home to Richmond, a friend asked if I knew how to code, because they were hiring and figured I would be good at it. A month later, I was inrolled at The University Of Richmond and loved everysecond of my new found "puzzles".
